I am a [M.S student](https://www.cs.cornell.edu/ms) at Cornell University studying Computer Science. My interests are algorithms, scientific computing, machine learning, and theory. 

I am currently working with [Alex Townsend](http://pi.math.cornell.edu/~ajt/) on solving Sylvester equations quickly by using randomized iterative methods inside the ADI algorithm. The project is co-led by [Robert Gower](https://gowerrobert.github.io/).

I received my B.S. from Cornell University as well, with minors in Mathematics and Electrical and Computer Engineering.

In my free time I enjoy [digital painting](https://www.instagram.com/pliplopleep/), swimming, and reading.
